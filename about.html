About

Furniture World opened its doors in
Montreal and allowed for its clients to see the
remarkable vision that Maria Di Pietro had
from the very beginning; a luxury furniture
and accessory store that distinguished itself
among the rest.

the store became a two-storey
showroom right on Decarie Boulevard,
making Furniture World a Montreal landmark.
Ten years later, the store has moved to an
even bigger showroom right across the street
in order to keep it's promise to deliver a wide
range of products and a space that will
continue to inspire.

Furniture World prides itself on the
commitment to be a leader in the industry,
while offering unparalleled customer service
at all times.
